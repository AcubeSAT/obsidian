## Admonition Plugin Tips

```ad-note
title: TOAST
collapse: open

**Toast** is sliced [bread](https://www.wikiwand.com/en/Bread "Bread") that has been [browned](https://www.wikiwand.com/en/Browning_(food_process) "Browning (food process)") by [radiant heat](https://www.wikiwand.com/en/Radiant_heat "Radiant heat"). The browning is the result of a [Maillard reaction](https://www.wikiwand.com/en/Maillard_reaction "Maillard reaction") altering the flavor of the bread and making it firmer. The firm surface is easier to spread toppings on and the warmth can help butter reach its [melting point](https://www.wikiwand.com/en/Melting_point "Melting point"). Toasting is a common method of making [stale bread](https://www.wikiwand.com/en/Staling "Staling") more [palatable](https://www.wikiwand.com/en/Palatability). Bread is toasted using a [toaster](https://www.wikiwand.com/en/Toaster "Toaster") or a [toaster oven](https://www.wikiwand.com/en/Toaster_oven "Toaster oven"). Toast may contain [carcinogens](https://www.wikiwand.com/en/Carcinogen#In_prepared_food) ([acrylamide](https://www.wikiwand.com/en/Acrylamide)) caused by the [browning process](https://www.wikiwand.com/en/Food_browning "Food browning").[^1]

[Butter](https://www.wikiwand.com/en/Butter "Butter") or [margarine](https://www.wikiwand.com/en/Margarine "Margarine"), and sweet toppings, such as [jam](https://www.wikiwand.com/en/Jam "Jam") or [jelly](https://www.wikiwand.com/en/Fruit_preserve#Jelly "Fruit preserve"), are commonly spread on toast. Regionally, savory spreads, such as [peanut butter](https://www.wikiwand.com/en/Peanut_butter "Peanut butter") or [yeast extract](https://www.wikiwand.com/en/Yeast_extract "Yeast extract"), may also be popular. Toast may accompany savory dishes such as soups or stews, or it can be topped with ingredients like eggs or [baked beans](https://www.wikiwand.com/en/Baked_beans "Baked beans") to make a light meal. Toast is a common [breakfast food](https://www.wikiwand.com/en/Breakfast_food "Breakfast food"). A [sandwich](https://www.wikiwand.com/en/Sandwich "Sandwich") may also use toasted bread.

[^1]: [tareke1](https://www.wikiwand.com/en/Toast_(food)#citenotetareke1)
```
